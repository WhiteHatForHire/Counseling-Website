My Philosophy

I believe that my job is to give my clients options: new ways to approach their issues, new perspectives, new tools, new knowledge, new ways to process trauma. And I try to do it with kindness, humor and respect. Because who doesn't want that? I am fascinated by the amazing ability of the human spirit to grow and change. In my practice, I do everything I can to facilitate the growth and change that my clients desire.
